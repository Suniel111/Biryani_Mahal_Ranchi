Chicken biryani is a popular and flavorful South Asian dish that combines aromatic rice with marinated chicken, spices, and herbs. It's known for its rich taste and complex layers of flavor. Biryani has many regional variations and can be found in different forms across countries like India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, and even in some Middle Eastern countries.
